📖 About Scheme

This poster promotes Airtel Payments Bank AEPS services including Aadhaar-based transactions and instant banking facilities.

It highlights easy account opening, UPI services, and passbook availability through Yojna Portal.

🧠 Relative Promt

Design a vertically oriented Indian government-style promotional banner (approx. size 1000x1280 px) with a clean, modern, high-contrast layout.

BACKGROUND:
Use a soft gradient background transitioning from light peach/orange at the top to white in the middle and slightly warm beige at the bottom. Add a faint circular watermark-style emblem in the center with very low opacity.

TOP SECTION:
At the very top, place a bold Hindi headline in large Devanagari font:
“ग्राहक सेवा केन्द्र”
Use thick, bold typography with white fill, strong red outline, and subtle black drop shadow for depth. Center-align the text.

Below it, place a small “YOJNA PORTAL” logo text in red and orange with a small icon-style mark to the left.

MAIN TITLE SECTION:
Below the logo, place the Airtel branding:

* A red Airtel logo symbol on the left
* Large bold English text “airtel Payments Bank” in red color, horizontally aligned

FEATURE BUTTONS:
Create two large rounded rectangular gradient buttons placed side-by-side:
Left button:
Text in Hindi: “तुरंत यूपीआई”
Right button:
Text in Hindi: “तत्काल पासबुक”
Buttons should have orange-to-red gradient fill, black border, and white bold text with slight shadow.

INFO TEXT SECTION:
Below the buttons, add a blue Hindi sentence:
“बच्चों का छात्रवृत्ति के लिए डीबीटी युक्त माइनर खाता”
Below that, add a red highlighted strip with Hindi text:
“आधार कार्ड से सभी बैंक खातों से पैसे निकाले/जमा जाते हैं.”

LEFT MIDDLE SECTION (AEPS):
Create a section titled “AEPS Service” in orange/black text.
Below it, place a smartphone mockup with screen text:
“Available Here
Aadhar ATM”
Below that include Aadhaar logo fingerprint graphic with “AADHAAR” text and AEPS logo.

To the left of the phone, vertically list icons with labels:

* Aadhaar Withdraw
* Aadhaar Payment
* Balance Enquiry
* Mini Statement
  Use simple flat icons in orange theme.

RIGHT MIDDLE SECTION:
Place Airtel Payments Bank logo again with Hindi text:
“एयरटेल का बैंक खाता”

BOTTOM RIGHT:
Add a smiling young Indian woman (professional look), wearing a green blazer, holding a card in her right hand (Airtel card). She should be facing forward slightly angled, clean studio lighting, soft shadow.

BOTTOM SECTION:
At bottom center-left, add website text:
“www.yojnaportal.com in bold black font.

FOOTER STRIP:
Add a red horizontal strip at the bottom with white Hindi text:
“APB बैंक का खाता खुलवायें , हाथो हाथ पासबुक पायें...”

TYPOGRAPHY & STYLE:

* Use bold, सरकारी-style fonts for Hindi
* Use clean sans-serif for English
* High contrast colors (red, orange, white, black)
* Add subtle drop shadows for depth
* Maintain structured layout with clear sections and spacing

Ensure all text remains exactly as written (Hindi in Hindi, English in English), and all elements are aligned cleanly with a professional promotional banner style.
