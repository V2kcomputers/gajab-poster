🧠 Relative Promt

Design a vertical government-style informational poster (approx. 1000x1280 px) with a clean, modern Indian service banner layout.

BACKGROUND:
Top section has a strong red-to-orange gradient background with a smooth blend. Middle section transitions into a light grey/white background. Bottom has a soft decorative gradient with faint floral/abstract shapes in pastel tones.

TOP HEADER:
At the very top, place a large bold Hindi heading in yellow with a slight dark outline/shadow:
"अपने किसी भी बैंक खाते में"
Text should be center-aligned, thick, and highly visible against the red-orange gradient.

Below this, place the NPCI logo on the left (blue text “NPCI” with tricolor arrow symbol) and next to it large bold green text:
"DBT/NPCI"
Under the NPCI logo, include small English text:
"National Payments Corporation of India"

To the right of the logo area, add a small branding text:
"YOJNA PORTAL" in orange/red modern font.

MAIN STRIP:
Below logos, add a light blue horizontal strip with bold Hindi text:
"डायरेक्ट बेनिफिट ट्रांसफर (DBT) चालू करवाएं"
Include a small circular refresh/loop icon on the left.

HIGHLIGHT BUTTONS:
Below the strip, create two rounded rectangular buttons side-by-side:
Left button (dark blue/purple): "तत्काल अपडेट"
Right button (dark blue/purple): "नया डीबीटी / बैंक बदलें"
Text should be bold yellow Hindi font.

CONTENT SECTION (LEFT SIDE):
Create a left-aligned bullet list with icons:

A mobile icon followed by:
"NPCI लिंकिंग / DBT अपडेट सेवा"
A light bulb icon:
"योजना का पैसा अब सीधे अकाउंट में"
A bank building icon:
"सरकारी पैसा सीधे बैंक खाते में पाएं"

DOCUMENT SECTION:
Add a rounded light yellow box with heading in Hindi:
"आवश्यक दस्तावेज"

Below it list items with green check icons:
✔ आधार कार्ड
✔ बैंक खाता
✔ मोबाइल नंबर

RIGHT SIDE HUMAN IMAGE:
Place a realistic smiling Indian woman on the right side (waist-up portrait). She has medium brown skin tone, curly dark hair, wearing a mustard/yellow kurta. She is holding a bank card towards the viewer. The card should have small text:
"BANK"
"Name Surname"
"XXXX-XXXX"
and a green label "DBT Done"
She should appear friendly and confident.

FOOTER:
At bottom-left, include website text in dark grey:
"www.yojnaportal.com
"

Below or near bottom, add a small strip with text:
"NPCI / DBT सेवा यहाँ उपलब्ध है"

STYLE & TYPOGRAPHY:
Use bold Hindi fonts for headings, सरकारी banner style typography. English text in clean sans-serif font. High contrast colors: yellow, green, blue, red. Soft shadows behind text and boxes for depth.

LAYOUT:
Balanced left-text and right-image composition. Clear hierarchy: header → logos → info strip → buttons → content → documents → footer.

Ensure spacing is neat, aligned, and poster looks like an official Indian government service advertisement.
