✨Mp Income and Domicile Key Highlights

  • Online application for Income & Domicile Certificate
  • Applicable for Madhya Pradesh residents
  • Useful for scholarships and government work
  • Simple and fast application process

📌 Important Details of Mp Income and Domicile

  • Samagra ID is mandatory
  • Aadhaar Card required
  • Mobile number needed for verification
  • Applicant photo and self-declaration form required
