🧠 Pan Card Apply / Banner Generation Prompt

Create a vertical government-style promotional banner (approx. 1000x1280 px) with a clean, modern, and semi-corporate Indian service design.

BACKGROUND:
Use a light grey to off-white gradient background with subtle abstract shapes. Add soft blue curved shapes and diagonal design accents in the top-right and bottom-left corners. Include small circular and dotted decorative elements in blue and orange scattered lightly across the background.

TOP SECTION:
At the top-left, place the English text "New Pancard" in a stylish italic or script-like font in purple color.
Below it, add a large bold Hindi title "पैन कार्ड" in dark blue with a thick white outline and slight drop shadow for depth.
Under this, include the text "Apply & Update" in bold orange gradient font with a slight 3D or shadow effect.
Near this heading area, place a small laptop icon (blue-themed) to the left side.

On the right side, place a smiling Indian man (young adult, wearing a white kurta) holding a PAN card toward the camera. The image should be well-lit, cutout style with soft shadow, placed overlapping the background but not blocking text.

Inside this strip, write bold white Hindi text:
"आवेदन, सुधार और प्रिंट"
The text should be centered and large, with strong contrast.

LEFT CONTENT SECTION:
