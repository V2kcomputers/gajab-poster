✨ Key Highlights

📌 Important Details

  • Verification will be done by Panchayat/Officials
  • Start toilet construction after approval
  • Amount will be credited after completion

📖 About Scheme

Under Phase-2 of the Pradhan Mantri Gramin Toilet Scheme, benefits are provided to families who were missed in the earlier BLS survey. The objective of this scheme is to ensure a toilet in every household and eliminate open defecation. Eligible beneficiaries receive financial assistance up to ₹12,000 directly in their bank accounts.
