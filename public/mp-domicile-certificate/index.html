✨ Key Highlights

  • Online application for MP Domicile Certificate
  • Required documents include Samagra ID and Aadhaar Card
  • eKYC of Samagra ID is mandatory
  • Useful for scholarship and government work

📌 Important Details

  • Applicant must have Samagra ID
  • Aadhaar Card is required for verification
  • Mobile number should be active for OTP
  • Applicant photo and self-declaration form needed

📖 About Scheme

MP Domicile Certificate is an important document used for proving residence in Madhya Pradesh.

It is required for school scholarships, government schemes, and various official purposes.
