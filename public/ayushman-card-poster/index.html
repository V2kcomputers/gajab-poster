✨ Key Highlights

  • Free healthcare coverage up to ₹5 lakh per family
  • Easy online application and card download
  • Available for eligible beneficiaries under PMJAY
  • Cashless treatment at empaneled hospitals

📌 Important Details

  • Aadhaar Card required
  • Mobile number required
  • Ration Card required
  • Or any other valid eligibility document

📖 About Scheme

Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY) provides free health insurance coverage up to ₹5 lakh for eligible families.

Beneficiaries can apply online and download their Ayushman Card to access cashless treatment services across India.
