🧠 Relative Promt

Create a vertical poster design (approx. 1000x1280) with a clean, soft light background (off-white/very light beige) and subtle faded circular shapes in pastel tones behind the main content for depth.

TOP SECTION:

* At the very top center, place a large, bold, golden 3D Hindi title “शादी कार्ड” with metallic gold gradient, embossed effect, soft highlights, and subtle shadow.
* On the top-left corner, add a small floral bouquet illustration with soft pastel colors (pink, peach, green).
* On both sides (left and right), place traditional Indian wedding cartoon characters (male and female) in orange/yellow attire holding musical instruments (shehnai style), facing inward.
* Below the title, place a small logo text “YOJNA PORTAL” in red and orange, centered.

MIDDLE SECTION (MAIN MESSAGE):

* Add a large Hindi sentence in dark green bold font, center aligned:
  “हमारे यहां सभी डिजाइन के शादी कार्ड छापे जाते हैं...”
* Font style should be thick, traditional Hindi, slightly rounded with soft shadow.

FEATURE STRIP (ICON ROW):

* Below the main text, create a horizontal light beige strip.
* Place three elements evenly spaced:

  1. Left: A red circular “BEST QUALITY” stamp badge.
  2. Center: A small illustration of a person holding multiple card designs with text “बेहतर डिजाइन”.
  3. Right: A yellow-red badge “BEST PRICE” with bold playful font and nearby Hindi text “सबसे कम रेट पर”.

LOWER CONTENT SECTION:

* Left side: Insert a rectangular wedding card sample image showing a bride and groom under a floral arch with vibrant colors (reds, greens, florals).
* Right side: Add a checklist with black bold Hindi text and check icons:
  ✔ सभी प्रकार के वेडिंग कार्ड डिजाइन
  ✔ हिंदी / इंग्लिश / कस्टम प्रिंटिंग
  ✔ लेजर प्रिंट / ऑफसेट प्रिंट
  ✔ डिजिटल कार्ड भी उपलब्ध
  ✔ कम कीमत में बेहतरीन क्वालिटी
* Add a large red exclamation mark “!” on the right side for emphasis.

CENTER FOOTER:

* Place website text “www.yojnaportal.com” in black, centered below the checklist.

BOTTOM STRIP (PROMO):

* Add a yellow patterned strip with small decorative dots.
* Include Hindi promotional text with emojis:
  “🎁 अभी बुकिंग पर पाएं विशेष छूट! 🎊 नए डिजाइन रोज उपलब्ध”

FINAL BOTTOM SECTION:

* Add a bold red Hindi sentence across full width:
  “हमारे यहां सभी प्रकार के शादी कार्ड , बेहतरीन डिजाइन और बहुत कम दाम पर छापे जाते हैं...”
* Left corner: small icon of a hand holding a gift box.
* Right corner: small wedding themed icon (hearts/rings or decorative symbol).

STYLE & DETAILS:

* Use clean, high-resolution vector style illustrations.
* Maintain balanced spacing and alignment.
* Use soft shadows and minimal depth effects.
* Typography should be bold, readable, and traditional Hindi style for main text.
* Colors: gold (title), green (main text), red/yellow (badges), beige background.
* Ensure all elements are centered and symmetrically arranged with a professional poster layout.
