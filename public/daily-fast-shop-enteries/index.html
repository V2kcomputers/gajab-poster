📖 About Scheme

This is a time-based entry sheet designed for small businesses to track daily sales and services efficiently.

It helps maintain organized records and improves financial monitoring throughout the day.

🧠 Relative Promt

A highly detailed, clean, and professional printable sheet design with a **white background** and a structured grid layout, titled **"Fast Entries"** at the **top center in bold black uppercase font** (simple sans-serif, सरकारी style, slightly larger than other text).

---

TOP SECTION:

* On the top row, aligned horizontally with proper spacing:

  * Left side: "Date:  /  /"
  * Center-left: "Opening Time:"
  * Center-right: "Closing Time:"
  * Right side: "Total Entries:" and "Total Got:"
* All header texts are in small black font, clean sans-serif, evenly spaced with no icons.

---

MAIN LAYOUT:

* The page is divided into a **grid of 12 equal rectangular boxes (3 rows × 4 columns)**
* Each box has:

  * **Black dashed border** (medium thickness, consistent spacing)
  * Light **off-white/very light grey background fill**
  * Inside each box, a **vertical dotted guideline** centered from top to bottom (light grey, thin)

---

TIME LABEL (TOP OF EACH BOX):

* Each box has a **light beige rectangular strip** at the top
* Inside the strip:

  * Time text in **bold black uppercase font**
  * Example text (exact formatting preserved):

    * "07.00 AM to 09.00 AM"
    * "09.00 AM to 10.00 AM"
    * "10.00 AM to 11.00 AM"
    * "11.00 AM to 12.00 PM"
    * "12.00 PM to 01.00 PM"
    * "01.00 PM to 02.00 PM"
    * "02.00 PM to 03.00 PM"
    * "03.00 PM to 04.00 PM"
    * "04.00 PM to 05.00 PM"
    * "05.00 PM to 06.00 PM"
    * "06.00 PM to 07.00 PM"
    * "07.00 PM to 09.00 PM"
  * The word **"to" is highlighted in orange color**, while the rest of the text is black
  * Text is center-aligned within the beige strip

---

BOTTOM STRIP (INSIDE EACH BOX):

* At the bottom of every box:

  * A **light green horizontal strip**
  * Left side text: **"Total:"**
  * Right side text: **"Entries:"**
  * Both texts are in bold black font
  * Clean spacing between left and right text, aligned horizontally
