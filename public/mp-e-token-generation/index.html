#MPYojna #EToken #KisanSeva
Easy Token Booking for Farmers – No Queue, No Hassle

✨ Key Highlights

  • Online e-token generation system for farmers
  • Managed by MP Agriculture Department
  • Quick access to fertilizers, seeds, and agri-services
  • No need to stand in long queues

📌 Important Details

  • Aadhaar Card is required
  • Farmer ID is mandatory
  • Mobile number needed for registration
  • Khasra (land record) details required

📖 About Scheme

This system helps farmers in Madhya Pradesh to easily book tokens online for fertilizer and agricultural services.

It ensures smooth distribution and avoids overcrowding by providing a digital token system.

🧠 Relative Promt

Design a vertical government-style informational poster (approx. 1000x1280 resolution) with a clean, structured layout and a soft light background. Use a subtle light beige to off-white gradient background with faint agricultural-themed watermark graphics (like crops or circular emblem shapes) in very low opacity.

TOP SECTION:
At the very top, place a bold Hindi heading in large green gradient font with black shadow and white stroke outline:
“वितरण एवं कृषि उर्वरक आपूर्ति समाधान”
The text should be center-aligned, thick, and सरकारी-style typography.

Below it, add a smaller subheading in dark brown/black Hindi font:
“संचालनालय किसान कल्याण तथा कृषि विकास, मध्य प्रदेश शासन”
Centered and neatly spaced.

Below that, place a small red “YOJNA PORTAL” logo/text in uppercase, centered.

MAIN TITLE SECTION:
Create a large central highlight title in bold yellow-golden gradient Hindi text with black shadow and green outline:
“ई टोकन जनरेशन”
This should be the most prominent element.

Below it, add:
“ई-विकास प्रणाली”
in medium-sized dark brown font, centered.

CTA BUTTON:
Under the title, place a rounded rectangular button in yellow with a blue inner text:
“ऑनलाइन रजिस्ट्रेशन”
The button should have soft shadow and look clickable.

Below the button, add the website in black:
“www.yojnaportal.com”

LEFT SECTION (DOCUMENT LIST):
Create a light green rounded box titled:
“आवश्यक दस्तावेज”
Below it, list bullet points with green arrow icons:
➤ आधार कार्ड
➤ फार्मर आई डी
➤ मोबाइल नंबर
➤ खसरे की जानकारी
Text should be bold and dark green.

Also include a circular green agriculture icon/logo on the left side (simple emblem style).

RIGHT SECTION (VISUAL OBJECT):
Place a realistic green fertilizer bag (3D rendered) on the right side.
The bag should have:

White center panel
QR code in the middle
Hindi text on bag:
“किसान कल्याण तथा कृषि विकास विभाग
मध्य प्रदेश शासन”
Bottom icons representing agriculture (leaf, plant symbols)

The bag should have realistic shading, folds, and a slight shadow beneath it.

BOTTOM SECTION:
Add a bold green Hindi sentence across the bottom:
“उर्वरक/खाद/बीज सेवाओं के लिए आसान टोकन बुकिंग - बिना लाइन, बिना परेशानी”
Use thick font with high contrast.

Decorate bottom corners with small grass/plant illustrations in soft green tones.

STYLE & DESIGN:

Use clean सरकारी poster style
Fonts: bold, high readability Hindi fonts
Colors: green (#2e7d32), yellow (#fbc02d), white, dark brown, light beige
Soft shadows under major elements
Balanced left-right composition (text left, object right)
Clear spacing and alignment
No extra elements beyond described content

Ensure the layout matches a professional Indian government scheme awareness banner.
