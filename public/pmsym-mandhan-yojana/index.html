✨ Key Highlights

  • ₹3000 monthly pension after 60 years of age
  • Applicable for unorganized sector workers
  • Low monthly contribution between ₹55 to ₹200
  • Government-backed pension scheme for financial security

📌 Important Details

  • Age eligibility: 18 to 40 years
  • Applicant must be working in the unorganized sector
  • Monthly income should be less than ₹15,000
  • Applicant should not be a member of EPFO, ESIC, or NPS

📖 About Scheme

This scheme is launched by the Government of India to provide pension benefits to workers in the unorganized sector.

After the age of 60, beneficiaries receive ₹3000 per month as pension under this scheme.
